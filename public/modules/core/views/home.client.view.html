What is Kali about?

Kali is a research project developed by the University of Rio Cuarto and Chair of Software Engineering at ETH Zurich. Our goal is to provide tool for organising testing competitions The tool displays:

  • List of bugs reported
  • Three types of ranking:
    • Users Ranking
    • Groups Ranking
    • Groups With More Bugs Ranking
The people behind Kali are Jorge Padula and Martin Nordio. Don't hesitate to get in touch with us.
